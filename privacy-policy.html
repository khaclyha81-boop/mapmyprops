Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 10, 2024

Introduction

MapMyProps ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Property preferences and search criteria
  • Financial information for mortgage pre-qualification
  • Communication records and feedback

Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and browser information
  • Device and usage data
  • Cookies and tracking technologies
  • Location data (with your consent)

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our real estate services
  • Connect you with appropriate real estate professionals
  • Send property alerts and relevant communications
  • Process transactions and provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Analyze website usage and improve user experience

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends.

Google Ads: We may use Google Ads for marketing purposes. Google may show our ads on other websites based on your previous visits to our site.

Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Real estate agents and mortgage lenders (with your consent)
  • Service providers and business partners
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Third parties in case of business transfer

Your Rights

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Control cookie preferences

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
