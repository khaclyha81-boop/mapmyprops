Fair Housing

Equal Housing Opportunity

MapMyProps is committed to providing equal housing opportunities to all individuals regardless of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, familial status, or disability. We support and comply with the Fair Housing Act and all applicable fair housing laws.

Protected Classes

Under federal law, it is illegal to discriminate in housing based on:

  • Race
  • Color
  • Religion
  • Sex (including gender identity and sexual orientation)
  • National origin
  • Familial status (families with children under 18)
  • Disability

Our Commitment

MapMyProps does not discriminate in the sale, rental, or advertising of dwellings. We are committed to:

  • Providing equal service to all clients
  • Ensuring all listings comply with fair housing laws
  • Training our staff on fair housing requirements
  • Maintaining accessible features for users with disabilities

Report Discrimination

If you believe you have experienced housing discrimination, you can file a complaint with:

U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD)

Phone: 1-800-669-9777

TTY: 1-800-927-9275

Website: www.hud.gov
