About MapMyProps

Your Trusted Real Estate Partner

MapMyProps is a leading real estate technology platform connecting buyers, sellers, renters, and real estate professionals across the United States. Founded in 2020, we've helped thousands of families find their perfect home through innovative technology and exceptional service.

Our platform combines cutting-edge map-based search technology with a comprehensive database of properties and experienced real estate agents to provide an unmatched home buying and selling experience.

Our Mission

"To democratize real estate by providing everyone with the tools, information, and connections needed to make informed property decisions with confidence."

What We Do

Property Search

Advanced map-based search with comprehensive filters to find your ideal home quickly and efficiently.

Agent Network

Connect with top-rated, licensed real estate professionals in your area who know the local market.

Market Analytics

Access real-time market data, price trends, and neighborhood insights to make informed decisions.

Mortgage Services

Connect with trusted lenders and get pre-approved for financing with competitive rates.
